# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Stellargrid to be a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone contributing — regardless of experience level, background, or how they found their way to GrantFox.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, PRs, and reviews.
- Assume good faith. Raise concerns directly with the person involved before escalating.
- Give and accept feedback gracefully — code review comments are about the code, not the person.
- Respect maintainers' and other contributors' time; keep discussion focused and on-topic.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks, public or private.
- Discriminatory jokes or language.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Sustained disruption of issues, PRs, or discussions.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces — issues, pull requests, code review, and any other communication channel used for Stellargrid contribution.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers via a GitHub issue (for non-sensitive matters) or through [GitHub's reporting tools](https://github.com/Thalamii/StellarGrid/security/advisories/new) for anything sensitive. Maintainers will review and respond to all reports, and may remove comments, close PRs, or restrict participation for violations.
